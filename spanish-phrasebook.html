🗣️ Spain Spanish Pronunciation Tips

  • C before E or I = “TH” sound (like “think”). Gracias → “GRAH-thee-as”
  • Z = always “TH” sound. Plaza → “PLAH-thah”
  • J = strong “H” sound from the throat. Hijo → “EE-hoh”
  • LL = “Y” sound. Calle → “KAH-yeh”
  • Ñ = “NY” sound. España → “es-PAH-nyah”
  • H = always silent. Hola → “OH-lah”
  • RR = rolled R. Practice by saying “butter” fast — the “tt” is close!
  • V = sounds like B. Vámonos → “BAH-moh-nos”
